What is Animation?
Animation is the technique of making inanimate objects or drawings appear to move in motion pictures or computer graphics.

Animation can be further divided into two which are;

  • Picture animation
  • Video animation
Picture animation
This is simply a method of uniquely enhancing a photo.

Video animation
This is a method in which pictures are manipulated to appear as moving images.

   Difference between picture & video animation
S/N Picture animation Video animation
1. It cannot move. It can move.
2. It makes no sound. It makes sound.
3. It serves as beautification. It serves as entertainment.
