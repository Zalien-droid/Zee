ZEE'S GUIDE ON ANIMATING PICTURES

Steps to create an animated picture
  • Download Autodesk sketchbook
  • Open the app.
  • Select the image.
  • Select your pen/pencil (preferably Technical pen).
  • Select color for sketching.
  • Start sketching.
  • On your fill.
  • Color your sketch using color picker.
  • Reduce the opacity to your satisfaction.
  • Rename the animated picture.
  • Save.
