ZEE'S GUIDE ON CREATING ANIMATED VIDEOS

Steps to create an animated video.
  • Download Plotagon story app.
  • Open the app.
  • Create your characters.
  • Choose your location.
  • Select the characters to be used in the selected location.
  • Position the characters.
  • Write what to say for each characters.
  • Select actions to be perfomed when necessary.
  • Add background music where needed.
  • Save your project.

Unfortunately I can't make already made animated video available on the site, but you can go check it on YouTube by searching "plotagon story animated videos"
